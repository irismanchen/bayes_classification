2007/08 UEFA Cup: Belenenses vs. Bayern Munich

Thursday, October 4, 2007 
Bayern Munich defeated Belenenses 2-0 (3-0 on Aggregate) to advance to the UEFA Cup Group Stage.
Bayern Munich, taking a 1-0 victory from the 1st leg, took a very defensive approach to the 2nd leg. Luca Toni opened the scoring in the 59th minute, which at this point sealed the aggregate victory with Belenenses needing 3 goals to advance. Hamit Altintop sealed the 2nd leg victory in the 77th minute.
Bayern Munich joins fellow Bundesliga clubs Nürnberg, Bayer Leverkusen and Hamburg in the Group Stage of this season's UEFA Cup.
