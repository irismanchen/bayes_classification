Tuesday, April 10, 2007Â 
South Africa beat West Indies by 67 runs in the Super 8 phase of the World Cup at the Cricket National Stadium, St. George's, Grenada.
This defeat all but eliminates hosts the West Indies, meaning the record of a host nation never winning the tournament would continue.
