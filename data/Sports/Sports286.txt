Antonio Filippo wins today's Omloop Het Volk

{date|March 3, 2007}} Today, &lt;a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Filippo_Pozzato"&gt;Filippo Pozzato&lt;/a&gt; (Team Liquigas) won the 62nd running of the Belgian distance bike race &lt;a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Omloop_%22Het_Volk%22"&gt;Omloop Het Volk&lt;/a&gt; with a sneak attack past the leaders, &lt;a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stuart_O%27Grady"&gt;Stuart O'Grady&lt;/a&gt; (Team CSC) and &lt;a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juan_Antonio_Flecha"&gt;Juan Antonio Flecha&lt;/a&gt; (Team Rabobank). O'Grady faded badly at the end, but Flecha hung on for second. &lt;a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tom_Boonen"&gt;Tom Boonen&lt;/a&gt; (Team Quickstep) from the chase group came third.
Mona Albano. "Omloop Het Volk cycling road race 2007" — Cycling TV, 2007-03-02
