Saturday, October 20, 2007Â 
Toronto FC ended their inaugural season with a 2-2 draw against New England Revolution.
The game was more evenly match as opposed from the 1st 2 games when New England Revolution won the 1st game 4-0 on April 14 and won the 2nd game 3-0. Michael Parkhurst opened the scoring with a goal from his own half just before halftime. Taylor Twellman doubled the Revolution lead immediately after the break off a corner kick. Toronto FC got a goal back about an hour into the game with Danny Dichio's goal in stoppage time in the 2nd half to secured a point for Toronto FC.
Toronto FC's season is finished as New England Revolution will face New York Red Bulls in the 1st Round of the playoffs.
