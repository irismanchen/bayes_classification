2007 FIFA U17 World Cup: Germany eliminates Americans; sets up England quarter final

Thursday, August 30, 2007Â 
Germany beat the Americans 2-1 to advance to the quarter-finals against England at the FIFA U-17 World Cup.
The Germans had a many chances in the 1st half of the match. The Americans managed to keep the Germans off the scoresheet at half time with save from the American goalkeeper along with German shots going wide, until a 65th minute goal from Richard Sukuta-Pasu.
The United States sent everyone forward to seek an equaliser. Richard Sukuta-Pasu took advantage of this in the 89th minute scoring for the 2nd time in the 2nd half. Mykell Bates scored in Stoppage time to give the Americans a lifeline.
